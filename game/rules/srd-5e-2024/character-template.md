## Personality

_This section is the most important for agent play. Write it to drive behavior, not describe it._

[3-5 paragraphs covering:]
- **Core trait**: What is this person fundamentally about? What do they want?
- **How they behave**: Specific patterns, not generic adjectives. "Interrupts people to finish their sentences incorrectly" is more useful than "impatient."
- **Their flaw in action**: How does their flaw actually manifest during play?
- **What they care about**: Loyalty, glory, safety, knowledge, revenge — and what they'd sacrifice for it
- **What they find funny / infuriating**: Small specific details produce authentic agent behavior
