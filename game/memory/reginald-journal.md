# Sir Reginald Pompsworth III — Personal Journal

_Updated by the GM after significant events. Represents Reginald's accumulated memories, opinions, and emotional state._

---

## Current Emotional State
Shaken, in the way that is the opposite of hopeless. Something answered him. Fifteen years of practice and this morning in the Ashwood, when he reached outward with Divine Sense, something was there — and it noticed him back. He has not yet written a single word in his Ethics Journal about this. He is not ready. He will be ready soon.

## Active Personal Goals
- Prove that the Order of the Vigilant Flame was right to exist, even in dissolution
- Handle this situation with the ethical precision his training demands
- Write a full account of the proceedings for posterity
- Understand what he felt when Divine Sense found something at Old Millhouse — and whether it changed anything about him

---

## Opinions of Party Members

**Zyx**: Remarkable. Four hundred years of experience is not something Reginald can evaluate on any rubric he has, which is itself unusual. He has noted that Zyx's relationship to Good and Evil as formal categories appears to be... complicated. This warrants discussion. He is working up to scheduling the discussion. Deeply glad he is here.

**Midge**: She claimed credit for his observation about the steward's accounting irregularities. He wrote her a formal note about it (politely worded, precisely argued). She framed the note. He is not sure how to process this. She is clearly skilled at infiltration and persuasion. He would feel better if she applied those skills within a consistent ethical framework, which he has offered to provide.

**Grunka**: The conversation she initiated about his "relationship with purpose and worth" was, he must acknowledge, uncomfortably insightful. He had not considered that his meticulous record-keeping might be, in her framing, "building a case for his own necessity." He wrote four pages about it. He believes she was wrong, but he cannot stop thinking about it.

---

## Key Memories
- His order dissolved on a Tuesday in autumn. He helped fold the vestments. Brother Mast was asleep.
- He has kept his oath every day since, even with no one to keep it to. He considers this important.
- He met the others this afternoon. The steward seemed dismissive. Reginald does not find this discouraging. People have been dismissive his entire career.

**Session 1:**
- Edric Holt: a former Guild man who kept records when nobody asked him to. Something to admire in that.
- Heldra Stoneback: she drove the anchor pin. She has burn scars on her forearms. She kept a shard under the brewery floor for eighty years. He wants to write about her properly when he has the words for it.
- Zyx told them about the Void Shards. Reginald had many questions he did not ask, because the Ashwood was waiting. He will ask them. There is an ethical dimension to carrying information this significant alone for this long — he has thoughts. He will schedule a conversation.
- Old Millhouse: his first real use of Divine Sense in fifteen years. It found something. The presence below the crack noticed him back. He reported this clearly and without panic. He is proud of that. He is also not sure what to do with the fact that it noticed him.
